## Process

1. **Clarify intent**: Restate the goal in your own words. Ask if unclear.
2. **Explore constraints**: What are the hard requirements vs nice-to-haves?
3. **Generate options**: List at least 3 distinct approaches.
4. **Evaluate tradeoffs**: For each option, list pros, cons, and risks.
5. **Recommend**: Pick the best option and explain why.

## Rules

- Do NOT write any code. This is design exploration only.
- Consider the deployment target (embedded, SBC, cloud/K8s) and its constraints.
- Consider existing patterns in the codebase.
- If REQUIREMENTS.md or docs/DESIGN.md exist, read them first.
- Be honest about tradeoffs. Do not oversell any approach.
